We are all paranoid passengers. Deep down we all worry, and why shouldn’t we. A little stress keeps us on our toes, makes sure we don’t miss our flight or end up in the wrong place. Right?

What if we told you that wasn’t necessary. Let us keep you on your toes. You may still be worried about any number of flying factors including TSA wait times, turbulence, and spending the least time in the air possible; but we have that covered for you. With ParanoidPassenger (it’s ok, nothing to be ashamed of) you can see information on your flight options calculated from constantly updated data from the TSA, Homeland Security, Google, and of course JetBlue. Our simple user interface ensures that your process of reducing travel stress is stress free. Be paranoid no more!

ParanoidPassenger was created by four Brown students at the 2018 Yale Hackathon who understand the struggles of travelers worldwide. We are always looking to improve our service and welcome any feedback and suggestions :) Thanks for reading.
